About Me

I'm curious and creative, and fascinated by computers and human psychology. I have so many questions about life, the universe, and everything!

  • Why do people's brains work the way they do?
  • Can we get computers to work in a similar way?
  • What is the purpose of emotions, and how can we get computers to recognize and understand human emotions, or even have emotions of their own?
  • How can we use technology to make beautiful and thought-provoking art and music?

But most urgently, I want to know the answer to the question, "what's next?"
